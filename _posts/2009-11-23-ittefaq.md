ittefaq se pyaar nahi hota,
na ittefaq se hoti hai koi mulaquaat.
bakht ho ya badnaseeb,
taqdeer kabhi ittefaq se nahi badal jaati,

humey jo ittefaq lagta hai,
wo bas khuda ki tarkeeb hoti hai.
