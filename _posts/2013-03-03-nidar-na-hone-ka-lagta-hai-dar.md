Dar se lagta hai darr,
Dar se lagta hai darr,
Dar par jeet na pane ka lagta hai darr
Nidar na hone ka lagta hai darr

Haar ka darr nahi to, jeet kaise paaunga
Jeet ka darr ho to, haar hi to paaunga

Darr bura hai, acha bhi hai,
Khota nahi kabhi, woh to sacha hai

Buzdil nahi, kaayar nahi, kamzoor bhi to main nahi
Nadaan tha to darr na tha, samajh se to badhti gayi

Raat se bhi darr nahi, dil ke andhere se lagta hai darr
Khudaa se to darr nahi, mazhab se lagta hai darr
Dushman se darr nahi, nadeem ko khone ka hai
Samajh se lagta hai dar, Samajh se lagta hai dar
Is jeev buddhi se lagta hai darr

Haar se nahi, Jeeth se nahi
Nidar na hone ka lagta hai darr
Nidar na hone ka lagta hai darr


---

**PURPORT**

I fear of fear, I fear of not winning over fear, not able to be fearless ... I

How will I win if I do not have fear of failure,
if I have fear of winning, I am sure to fail ... II

fear is bad, good as well, but never a lie, fear is truth ... III

I am not weak-hearted or coward or a weak person,
I was fearless when  I was innocent, fear grew with consciousness ... IV

I do not fear night, I fear the darkness of my heart
I do not fear god, I fear religion,
I do not fear enemies, I fear loosing my best friends
I fear intelligence, I fear this intelligent human ... V

I fear not of loosing,
I fear not of winning,
I fear of not being fearless
I fear of not being fearless. ... VI
